About Us

Every Stitch Tells a Story.

Lolasi Empire is a premium fashion brand built on passion, creativity, excellence, and purpose. Founded by Fasasi Funmilola Ayomide, our journey began with humble beginnings and an unwavering commitment to mastering the art of fashion.

Today, we have grown into a trusted brand known for elegant craftsmanship, attention to detail, and exceptional customer experience. Every garment we create is thoughtfully designed to make our clients feel confident, beautiful, and unforgettable.

Beyond creating fashion, we are passionate about empowering people with practical skills through Lolasi Fashion Academy, raising a new generation of skilled designers who will shape the future of the fashion industry.

— Fasasi Funmilola Ayomide, Founder

Lolasi Fashion Academy

Learn. Create. Lead.

Lolasi Fashion Academy is the educational arm of Lolasi Empire, established to train aspiring fashion designers with practical, industry-standard skills and an entrepreneurial mindset.

Our goal is not simply to teach sewing — we equip students with the creativity, technical expertise, discipline, and confidence needed to build successful careers in fashion.

    Our Story

    Every Great Brand Begins With A Dream.

    2016

    While studying at university, our founder began learning fashion beside a roadside tailor — humble beginnings that became the foundation of a remarkable journey defined by resilience and determination.

    After Graduation

    She attended a professional fashion school and gained additional practical experience to refine her craft.

    2022

    Upon completing the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC), Lolasi Empire was officially launched.

    Today

    The brand represents excellence, innovation, and purpose — creating beautiful garments while empowering future designers through Lolasi Fashion Academy.

    Our Vision

    To become one of Africa's most admired fashion brands, recognized for exceptional craftsmanship, innovative designs, and transformative fashion education that empowers individuals and communities.
